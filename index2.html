Jazz Band

From TEMS Jazz Band to The Taft School Jazz Band

I started playing trombone in 4th grade. When I went to middle school I joined the Jazz Band and played the lead trombone part. Directed by Mr. Frank Marci our band went on to be the first middle school band to play live at a New York Rangers Game. We also placed second for middle school bands at the National Jazz Festival in Philadelphia. The Taft Jazz Band directed by Mr. TJ Thompson plays frequent shows at churches and fundraising events. We are going on tour the on April 23-25 in Boston. We will play at Symphony Hall and play a church service.
